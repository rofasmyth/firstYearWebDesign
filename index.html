What is this page?

The museum has many items which are not on 
display due to space constraints and the 
difficulty of preserving exhibits.    
As such, through this webpage, the public 
is able to view the items which we 
have but cannot display on the museum floor.
Furthermore, should any member of the public
have similar historical materials 
at home, they can upload a digital copy to the
museum through this machine. This can be 
done by using a pen drive and the 
submission page.
          
Thank you for your visit and your support.
